Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps, used solely for security and debugging.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To display your expense history and summaries.
  • To authenticate you securely when you log in.
  • To diagnose errors and improve the reliability of the Service.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the Service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, but no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties. We may use limited third-party infrastructure services (e.g., hosting providers) solely to operate the Service. These providers are contractually bound to handle your data only as directed by us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.

To exercise any of these rights, please use the support contact listed in the app.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the support contact listed in the app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
